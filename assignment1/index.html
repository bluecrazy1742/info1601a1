About Us

YardHarvest Market is a local farmers market dedicated to providing fresh, locally-sourced produce to our community. We work closely with both local farmers and large to ensure that our customers have access to the best quality fruits and vegetables. Our mission is to support local agriculture and that extends to both the small family owned farms and the larger farms that have been around for decades. We are committed to sustainability and working with our farmers to ensure that our produce is grown using environmentally-friendly practices.
